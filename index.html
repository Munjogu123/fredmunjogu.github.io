Projects

Product Sales Data Preparation and Cleaning

This project analyzes sales performance following the launch of a new office stationery product line. Using customer data, we explored the effectiveness of three sales methods: email, call, and a combination of both, by examining metrics such as revenue distribution, customer engagement, and weekly revenue trends. Through data cleaning, visualizations, and exploratory analysis, we found that the email and call method consistently generated the highest revenue over time, while the email-only method saw an early peak followed by decline. Based on these insights, we recommend prioritizing the email and call strategy to maximize revenue and improve customer reach.
